ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Dami, a proud Nigerian studying computer science at Dixie State University, Utah. I enjoy intricately learning technology I am interested in, it's my little way of appreciating their contribution to the world of programming. I love talking and thinking about how we can make programming for the web a little bit easier, anchoring on the kindness and supportive nature of the JavaScript ecosystem.

I spend most of my free time exploring the beautiful world of JavaScript, and its limitless possibilities. I am passionate about music, and I could go on forever about social change in my country, Nigeria. There is just so much in me for one life.

I plan on venturing into Machine Learning(training computers to be as smart and in certain cases smarter than humans by feeding them lots of data which counts as experience), and Natural Language Processing(training computers to understand human language).

There is so much happening in the world today; good, bad, and really horrible stuff. One of my long-term goals is to tell stories of these things in order to spike conversations and profer solutions.
