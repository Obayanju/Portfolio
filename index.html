ABOUT ME

My Name is Obayanju Damilare. You can call me Dami. I am a Nigerian currently studying Computer Science in Dixie State Universitiy, Utah. I have a variety of interests ranging from analyzing and predicting films to animations to computer programming. Programming, however, does peak my interests.

In the future, I hope to be well versed in web development, Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing(I'll love to create a google assistant translator in my language, Yoruba). I'll also love to write movie scripts sometime.
